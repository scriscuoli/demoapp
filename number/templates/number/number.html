Magic 6174
Discovered by Indian mathematician D. R. Kaprekar in 1949,
this number acts as a "mathematical black hole" for four-digit numbers.
If you apply a specific set of rules called Kaprekar's Routine, every
four-digit number (as long as it has at least two unique digits)
will eventually collapse into 6174 within seven steps.

Min = your four digit number (with leading zeros as necessary) with its digits in ascending order
Max = your four digit number (with leading zeros as necessary) with its digits in descending order
The next number in the sequence is the result of substracting Min from Max.
The process repeats (no more than 7 times) until 6174 is reached.
